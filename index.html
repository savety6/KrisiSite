Athlete, proffesional table tennis player

Kristina Vramencalieva is moving from Bulgaria to Thannhausen. The 21-year-old national player is currently number 523 on the world ranking and is regularly in action internationally. She competes in the world championships or qualifications . Over the past three years, she won four out of four possible medals at the Bulgarian U21 Championships. One of her greatest successes was the Team World Cup 2018 in Sweden, when she convinced Bulgaria with plus balance in all games."
